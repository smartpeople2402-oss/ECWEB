Apa itu Conjunction?

Bayangkan kalimat adalah batu bata. Conjunction (Kata Sambung) adalah semen atau lem yang merekatkannya.


Tanpa conjunction, kita akan berbicara seperti robot:

  • ‚ùå Tanpa Conjunction: "I like coffee. I like tea. I don't like milk."
  • ‚úÖ Dengan Conjunction: "I like coffee and tea, but I don't like milk."

Coordinating Conjunctions (FANBOYS)

Ini adalah jenis paling dasar. Mereka menghubungkan dua hal yang setara (Kata dengan Kata, atau Kalimat dengan Kalimat). Hafalkan akronim FANBOYS:

Kata (Acronym) Arti & Fungsi Contoh
For Karena (Formal) I drank water, for I was thirsty.
And Dan (Penambahan) I like apples and oranges.
Nor Ataupun tidak (Negatif) I don't like tea, nor do I like coffee.
But Tetapi (Kontradiksi) Small but strong.
Or Atau (Pilihan) Tea or Coffee?
Yet Namun (Kejutan) He is rich, yet he is unhappy.
So Sehingga (Hasil) It rained, so I stayed home.

Advanced Connections

Selain FANBOYS, ada dua jenis penghubung lain yang membuat kalimat Anda lebih kompleks dan "pintar".

1. Subordinating (Hubungan Sebab/Waktu)

Menghubungkan induk kalimat dengan anak kalimat. Kalimat ini menunjukkan hubungan sebab-akibat atau waktu.

  • Reason: Because, Since, As.
  • Time: When, While, Before, After.
  • Condition: If, Unless.
  • Contrast: Although, Even though.
  • Contoh: I will go if you go.

2. Correlative (Pasangan Setia)

Kata sambung yang selalu berpasangan. Tidak boleh dipisahkan atau ditukar pasangannya.

  • Both ... and ... (Keduanya)
  • Either ... or ... (Salah satu)
  • Neither ... nor ... (Tidak keduanya)
  • Not only ... but also ... (Tidak hanya... tapi juga)
  • Contoh: Both you and I are happy.
PRO TIP: ALTHOUGH VS BUT

Jangan pernah menggunakan "Although" dan "But" dalam satu kalimat. Pilih satu saja!

  • ‚ùå Although he is sick, but he works. (Salah/Redundan)
  • ‚úÖ Although he is sick, he works.
  • ‚úÖ He is sick, but he works.

Conjunction Knowledge Check

1. I wanted to buy the shoes, ______ I didn't have enough money.

2. You can have ______ tea or coffee. (Pilih salah satu)

3. It was raining hard, ______ we cancelled the picnic.

4. ______ John nor Mary came to the party. (Tidak keduanya)

5. She is smart, ______ she is lazy. (Kejutan/Kontras)

6. He is ______ handsome but also kind.

7. I will call you ______ I arrive home. (Waktu)

8. ______ she was tired, she kept working. (Kontras)

9. I study hard ______ I want to pass the exam.

10. Would you like tea ______ coffee? (Pilihan)
