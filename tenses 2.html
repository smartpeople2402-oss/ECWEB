Apa itu Tenses 2?

Tenses 2 berfokus pada bentuk waktu yang lebih kompleks, yaitu menggabungkan aspek Continuous (sedang berlangsung) dan Perfect (sudah selesai) dalam kerangka waktu Past (Lampau) dan Future (Masa Depan).


Tenses ini membantu kita menjelaskan:

  • Kejadian yang sedang berlangsung di titik tertentu di masa lalu/depan.
  • Kejadian yang selesai sebelum kejadian lain terjadi di masa lalu/depan.

Past Continuous Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang sedang berlangsung di masa lampau pada waktu tertentu, atau saat kejadian lain menyela.

FUNGSI UTAMA
  • Sedang berlangsung di masa lalu: "I was studying at 8 PM last night."
  • Dua kejadian (satu menyela): "I was cooking when my mom called."

Rumus & Contoh

Jenis Kalimat Rumus Contoh
Positif (+) S + was/were + V-ing + O She was reading a book.
Negatif (-) S + was/were + not + V-ing + O They were not playing football.
Tanya (?) Was/Were + S + V-ing + O? Were you watching TV?

Time Signals: At this time yesterday, Last night, When, While.

Future Continuous Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang akan sedang berlangsung di masa depan pada waktu tertentu.

FUNGSI UTAMA
  • Sedang berlangsung di masa depan: "I will be studying at 8 PM tomorrow."
  • Rencana masa depan: "They will be traveling to Bali next month."

Rumus & Contoh

Jenis Kalimat Rumus Contoh
Positif (+) S + will be + V-ing + O He will be playing football tomorrow.
Negatif (-) S + will not be + V-ing + C They will not be studying tonight.
Tanya (?) Will + S + be + V-ing + O? Will you be waiting for me?

Time Signals: Tomorrow at this time, Next week, Tonight at 9 PM.

Past Perfect Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang telah selesai sebelum kejadian lain di masa lampau.

FUNGSI UTAMA
  • Selesai sebelum kejadian lain: "I had finished my homework before my mom came."
  • Selesai sebelum waktu tertentu: "She had left the office by 5 PM."

Rumus & Contoh

Jenis Kalimat Rumus Contoh
Positif (+) S + had + V3 + O She had finished the report.
Negatif (-) S + had not + V3 + O They had not visited Bali before last year.
Tanya (?) Had + S + V3 + O? Had you called him before you arrived?

Time Signals: Before, After, By the time, Already, Just.

Future Perfect Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang akan sudah selesai sebelum waktu tertentu di masa depan.

FUNGSI UTAMA
  • Selesai sebelum waktu tertentu: "I will have finished my homework by 8 PM."
  • Selesai sebelum kejadian lain: "She will have arrived before the meeting starts."

Rumus & Contoh

Jenis Kalimat Rumus Contoh
Positif (+) S + will have + V3 + O He will have finished the project by tomorrow.
Negatif (-) S + will not have + V3 + O They will not have completed the task.
Tanya (?) Will + S + have + V3 + O? Will you have graduated by next year?

Time Signals: By tomorrow, By next week, By the end of this year, Before.
