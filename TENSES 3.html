Apa itu Tenses 3?

Tenses 3 adalah tingkatan lanjut yang berfokus pada bentuk Perfect Continuous. Bentuk ini menggabungkan aspek "sudah terjadi" (Perfect) dan "sedang berlangsung" (Continuous).


Tenses ini sangat penting untuk:

  • Menekankan durasi suatu kejadian.
  • Menyatakan kejadian yang dimulai di masa lalu dan masih berlangsung hingga titik waktu tertentu (sekarang, masa lalu, atau masa depan).

Present Perfect Continuous

Digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang sudah dimulai di masa lampau dan masih berlangsung hingga sekarang, atau baru saja selesai dengan menekankan durasinya.

Rumus & Contoh

Jenis Kalimat Rumus Contoh
Positif (+) S + have/has + been + V-ing She has been reading for two hours.
Negatif (-) S + have/has + not + been + V-ing They have not been working since morning.
Tanya (?) Have/Has + S + been + V-ing? Have you been waiting long?
TIME SIGNALS & FUNGSI

Waktu: Since (sejak), For (selama), Lately (akhir-akhir ini), Recently, All day.

Fungsi Utama:
1. Kejadian masih berlangsung (Learning English for 3 years).
2. Kejadian baru saja selesai (Cleaning house -> Tired).
3. Menekankan durasi (Waiting since morning).

Past Perfect Continuous

Digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang sedang berlangsung di masa lampau dan berlangsung selama periode waktu tertentu sebelum kejadian lain terjadi di masa lampau.

Rumus & Contoh

Jenis Kalimat Rumus Contoh
Positif (+) S + had been + V-ing They had been playing football for two hours before it rained.
Negatif (-) S + had not been + V-ing He had not been studying when the teacher arrived.
Tanya (?) Had + S + been + V-ing? Had she been working there for a long time?
TIME SIGNALS & FUNGSI

Waktu: For, Since, Before, When, Until, How long.

Fungsi Utama:
1. Berlangsung sebelum kejadian lain (Studying before mom arrived).
2. Menekankan durasi di masa lalu (Living there for ten years before moving).
3. Hasil dari kejadian masa lalu (Tired because had been working).

Future Perfect Continuous

Digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang akan sudah berlangsung selama periode waktu tertentu di masa depan, sebelum kejadian lain terjadi atau sebelum waktu tertentu.

Rumus & Contoh

Jenis Kalimat Rumus Contoh
Positif (+) S + will have been + V-ing He will have been working here for 10 years by next month.
Negatif (-) S + will not have been + V-ing They will not have been studying for long by then.
Tanya (?) Will + S + have been + V-ing? Will you have been waiting for two hours by the time I arrive?
TIME SIGNALS & FUNGSI

Waktu: By next month, For, By the time, Before.

Fungsi Utama:
1. Menunjukkan durasi di masa depan (Working for 5 years by next year).
2. Aktivitas sebelum kejadian lain (Driving for 6 hours before reach city).
3. Prediksi kondisi (Cooking all day -> will be tired).
